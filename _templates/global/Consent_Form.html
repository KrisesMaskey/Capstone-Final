Welcome to this study in which you will do an activity with other anonymous participants online in real time. The study is led by Talal Rahwan, an associate professor of Computer Science at New York University Abu Dhabi.

Should you choose to participate, you will do the following:

  1. Read and understand the rules of the activity.
  2. Answer comprehension check questions to test your understanding of the rules. If you fail twice to correctly answer all questions, you will not be allowed to participate, but we will compensate you with ${{ failed_compensation }} for your time.
  3. Wait for the other participant to join the activity.
  4. Participate in the activity.
  5. Answer follow up questions.
